Sangolli Rayanna

The great freedom fighter

Sangolli Rayanna (15 August 1796 – 26 January 1831) was an Indian military
Shetsanadi (Sainik) and warrior in the Kittur princely state of the Karnataka.
He was the Shetsanadi of the Kingdom of Kittur ruled at the time by Rani Chennamma
and fought the British East India Company till his death.
